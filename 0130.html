Flash Android TV 7 S905x (Sunvell T95x)

DISCLAIMER: This process will overwrite the current Android ROM on your TV box. I have tested these steps on my own devices, but I am not responsible if you damage or brick your device. Verify the ROM will work on your device before starting and make sure to backup any data you do not want to lose before proceeding.

  1. Download Aidan's Android TV ROM v7.1 Download
  2. Download Amlogic Burning Tool v2.2.0 Download
  3. Optional, Download Magisk v20.4 for root access Download
    1. Magisk must be installed from TWRP recovery if root access is desired
  4. Extract Amlogic Burning Tool v2.2.0
  5. Install Amlogic Burning Tool v2.2.0
  6. Launch Amlogic Burning Tool v2.2.0
  7. Go to File > Import Image > Browse to and select Aidan's Android TV ROM .img file
  8. Click the Start button in the top right corner of the application
  9. Connect the S905x TV box to the PC via USB cable
  10. Press the reset button (sometimes hidden inside the AV port)
  11. Wait for the ROM to copy over to the device
  12. Once the process completes (100%: Burning Successfully) click the Stop button
  13. Disconnect the TV box from the PC USB
  14. Connect the TV box to an HDMI display and power it on
  15. Enjoy a clean and fast Android TV experience on your S905x device
